Pedalto Emissary Program














The Pedalto Emissary program is a free* diplomatic program for the creative public. Often times artists, writers, musicians, and random eccentrics need someone to represent them in places where they are not. The Pedalto Emissary Program assigns aesthetic producers with an emissary who will complete a mission that is requested to them. Simply fill out the mission request form below and a member of the Pedalto Institution will shortly contact you about emissary placement. At the successful completion of your mission a form with documentation will be emailed to you.


*for commercial services and expedited executions a fee will apply. Representee covers all expenses for mission.  See full rules and regulations
