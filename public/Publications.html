PUBLICATIONS

Bureaucratia necessitas non est, amor est

Pedalto Fellows produce an impressive body of books, essays, and in-depth articles that explore innovative ideas with the potential to transform the incorporated arts. But unless those ideas are widespread, the opportunity to have an impact is lost. Consequently, the Institution disseminates its products through varied formats and means. Currently available to the general public are three Pedalto pubications, two annual reports and a prospectus, all from 2001-2002. To obtain a copy please email hqlatimer@pedalto.org
