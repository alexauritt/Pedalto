tomorrow is the board meeting for ralph lauren. I have been given one

invite and am under strict instructions to only bring people with

money, or come alone. Oh well. I may go to this, but I have found my

reunion with columbia university's main library has been a much more

enriching way to spend my evenings uptown as opposed to downtown or in

midtown. So we will see. Let me know if you want to come, and have

some way to convince my friend, some sort of i banker/investor type

from russia.


Also, for your sake, I was trying to make a deal with this guy, some

kind of french, also i banker/promoter type, that seems to get some

very good party lists. Weekdays, all the hottest parties and events

(except for tonites cassio party with kanya west at ciprianis

downtown, he didnt seem to get on the list for that one) and on

weekends upscale apartment parties.


Speaking of upscale apartments, back in the good old days when I had

my lovely apartment at 21a, http://www.1600broadway.com (i said it was

ph, but the 6 ph floors---you know everyman a king with 7 acres a mule

and 1000 square feet with a balcony--- A.N. his initials, always

seemed to show up at my parties.


Post penthouse days things seemed to change. Firstly, he and his gf

had a big breakup. But somehow she and i had quite the rapport,

partying a lot, even introducing her to a media mogul...it turned out

he had all these party lists but was very very machievelian in who he

gave his info to. One homeless party crasher, the infamous wg, AN

didnt care how many lives WG messed with, as long as he got some

kernals of info out of him he would supply him with steady stream of

events to crash.


Its late and i have to sleep, so ill try to truncate this story, we

had discussed some sort of exchange, i cant say what,  because AN

would be super pissed about that, but its a very basic sort of

exchange, all capitalist societies use this medium as the key means of

exchange, so there was to be some of that involved. I was supposed to

bring my cows to his market in exchange for his c list, more cows for

his b list and even more cows, vegan that i am, for his a list.

Imagine the french accent emphasizing each gradation of list (remember

that for later on in the story).


Today he posted as he always does a little after the fact, all the

parties he went to on facebook. Suddenly, as i was leaving the stacks

in the main library of columbia, feeling super enriched, and during

that time of semi scholarly enrichment there was no cell phone

reception i get this text to my cell phone: "NEVER, EVER post about

current and future events on my wall." I didnt know this was one of

his rules. Its so soup naziesque. Isnt it?? I remember being in a taxi

with the crashers, a group of utterly unattractive people who get in

everywhere, and on that night i thought instead of hating them, id

hang with them and he kept texting them, several of them "if you cant

keep supplying me with events, you will no longer be on my a list of

events, and maybe not even on my c list." Remember all from the thick

french accent.....


Ah, the cost of partying in new york.
